How to Play
Setup

Before play begins, players must deploy all ships from their fleet onto the grid. Each ship occupies a number of consecutive squares on the grid, either horizontally or vertically. Ships cannot overlap or be placed diagonally. Both players will have the same fleet makeup.

Gameplay

After all ships have been deployed, the game proceeds by round. Each round, players will have a turn to select a square at which to fire a shot. If that square is occupied by a ship, it is a "hit" (red marker) and the player will be rewarded with another shot. Otherwise, it is a "miss" (white marker) and the player's turn will end.

Winning

Sink all of the ships in your opponent's fleet to win! But if your opponent sinks all of your ships first, you will lose.
